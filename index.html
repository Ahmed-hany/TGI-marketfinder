How it works
▼
1
Select Market via Footer Tabs or Left Panel
The 5 priority markets are pinned in the footer bar at the bottom of the screen. Click any country tab to pre-fill the market. You can also enter any country in the world using the Configure panel on the left.
2
Review the Competitive Landscape
The Competitor Analysis section above gives you the global category benchmark — Chili's as primary threat, plus all other American casual dining brands with threat ratings, positioning metrics, and TGI differentiators. The live analysis will add market-specific data.
3
Review the Scope
The Research, GTM, and Creative tabs show all 14 modules that will be included in every analysis — market intelligence, competitive analysis, franchise economics, partner directory, outreach sequences, and bilingual creative.
4
Generate — Claude Researches Live
Claude conducts real-time web research — current market data, Brinker financials, franchise operators, PE activity, events. No static reports. 2–4 minutes for a full analysis.
5
Word Document Saved to Workspace
A complete formatted .docx is saved to your TGI Projects folder — includes all selected sections, partner directory, 5-touch outreach sequences, bilingual creative, and 30–60 day action plan.
